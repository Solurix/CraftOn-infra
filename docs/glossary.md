# Glossary — Japanese construction & domain terms

For AI sessions and non-Japanese contributors. These terms appear throughout the docs
and the product domain.

## People & roles
- **職人 (shokunin)** — skilled tradesperson / craftsman. The "worker" side.
- **一人親方 (hitori-oyakata)** — sole proprietor tradesman (works for himself; treated as
  a business, not an employee). Contract = subcontract, not employment.
- **親方 (oyakata)** — lead/master tradesman; may hire others.
- **現場監督 (genba-kantoku)** — site supervisor / foreman. Core "contractor" persona.
- **施工管理技士 (sekō-kanri-gishi)** — licensed construction management engineer.
- **工務店 (kōmuten)** — small/mid construction firm. The paying customer.
- **元請 / 下請 (motouke / shitauke)** — prime contractor / subcontractor.

## Labor & contracts
- **スポット / オンデマンド** — spot / on-demand (single-day, immediate) work.
- **常用 (jōyō)** — day-rate regular work (vs. lump-sum subcontract).
- **日雇い直接雇用 (hiyatoi chokusetsu koyō)** — day-labor direct employment. The
  Timee-style contract for the `employee` route.
- **業務委託 / 請負 (gyōmu-itaku / ukeoi)** — subcontract / contract-for-work. The
  `freelance` route.
- **飛び / ドタキャン (tobi / dotakyan)** — no-show / last-minute cancellation. The #1
  pain the app prevents.
- **中抜き (nakanuki)** — disintermediation; parties going around the platform to deal
  directly. The app actively prevents this.
- **手元 / 雑工 (temoto / zakkō)** — unskilled helper work.

## Trades (examples)
- **大工 (daiku)** — carpenter.
- **左官 (sakan)** — plasterer.
- **とび / 鳶 (tobi)** — scaffolder / steeplejack.
- **型枠 (katawaku)** — formwork (concrete molds).
- **墨出し (sumidashi)** — layout marking on site.

## Compliance, safety, tax
- **在留カード (zairyū card)** — residence card (foreign residents). Front+back upload
  required; visa expiry tracked.
- **在留資格 (zairyū shikaku)** — residence/visa status (defines work permission).
- **不法就労助長罪** — crime of facilitating illegal work. The "one-strike = shutdown" risk.
- **グリーンサイト (Green Site)** — labor/safety document management system for sites.
- **CCUS (建設キャリアアップシステム)** — Construction Career Up System; national registry
  of workers' history & qualifications.
- **労災 (rōsai)** — workers' accident compensation insurance.
- **一人親方労災 (特別加入)** — special voluntary 労災 enrollment for sole proprietors.
- **源泉徴収 (gensen-chōshū)** — withholding tax. 丙欄 (heiran) = the day-labor withholding
  column/table.
- **インボイス** — qualified invoice (consumption-tax system).
- **KY活動 (KY-katsudō)** — hazard-prediction (Kiken Yochi) safety activity; daily safety
  sheet.
- **マイナンバー** — the 12-digit national ID **number** (avoid storing).
  **マイナンバーカード** — the physical card (can be used for auth via JPKI without the number).
- **JPKI (公的個人認証)** — Japanese Public Key Infrastructure; card-based identity verification.
- **eKYC** — electronic Know-Your-Customer (online identity verification).
- **APPI (個人情報保護法)** — Japan's personal-information protection law.

## Money / platform
- **手数料 (tesūryō)** — fee / commission.
- **掛け払い (kakebarai)** — deferred B2B invoice payment (month-end close, pay next month).
- **ファクタリング** — factoring; here, B2B payment guarantee for receivables.
- **首都圏 (shutoken)** — Greater Tokyo metropolitan area. Launch region.
